About this standard

Owner Professional Record Standards Body
Status Active

What this status means

Active standards are stable, maintained and have been assured or endorsed for use by qualified bodies.

Draft standards are still being developed or are waiting for assurance or endorsement by qualified bodies.

Deprecated standards aare older versions of a standard which are being phased out.

Retired standards are not being maintained and should not be used.
