Mental health inpatient discharge

Standardising information shared so professionals can provide continuity of care when an adult is discharged from mental health services. Includes information on patient history, medications, as well as current and previous diagnoses.

Relationships to other standards

Dependencies This standard needs to be reviewed and implemented alongside SNOMED, FHIR and electronic systems on both ends.
Related standards This standard belongs to the group of transfers of care record standards, including eDischarge and Emergency care discharge summaries, Outpatient letters, Personalised care and support plan and Core information standard.

Assurance and endorsements

Legally mandated Yes
Required under: Section 250 Health and Social Care Act

What this requirement means

The Health and Social Care Act 2012 is an act of the Parliament of the United Kingdom. It provides for the most extensive reorganisation of the structure of the National Health Service in England to date.
Quality assured by: Data Alliance Partnership Board

Reference code: DCB1234

About the Data Alliance Partnership Board

The Data Alliance Partnership Board (DAPB) is England's national body overseeing standards development in health and adult social care. Standards assured by the DAPB have been assessed against strict criteria to ensure they're appropriate for use across England's care settings.
