Creating a single patient record: Bradford

Creating a single patient record for GPs, hospitals, adult social care teams and other local services.

Background

We first started thinking about how to join up care across our local area in 2008. We’d won some funding from a government scheme to support innovation in healthcare technology and we put it towards a pilot project connecting the GP services and community care.

Problems to solve

Not being able to access up to date records for people using health and care services was causing a lot of headaches for staff. Staff were afraid about missing crucial details about someone’s background that meant they didn’t provide the right treatment or service.

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges has been around people being able to use the technology. I’d recommend choosing IT products where there’s a big emphasis on how easy they are to use and where they’ve actually tested the products with real people. What we’ve found is if the system isn’t really simple and intuitive people tend not to want to use it or they need training, which can be hard to organise in busy healthcare settings.
