  • International statistical classification of disease and health related problems (ICD) 5th Edition

    ICD-10, Edition is the current, approved standard for use in England. ICD permits the systematic analysis, interpretation and comparison of morbidity data collected in different areas. The specified purpose of the ICD-10 is to provide a means of classifying diagnoses and is defined as a system of categories to which morbid entries are assigned according to established criteria.
