Mental health inpatient discharge

Use this care record to collect consistent information when discharging an adult patient from a mental health care setting - such as a hospital or community team - to their GP practice.

View this standard on Professional Record Standards Body's website

Category Content format
Status Active
Compliance Recommended for use on relevant services

Version 2.1 (latest version)
Date published 14 April 2020
Release notes

The Mental health inpatient discharge has been updated to version 2.1 (Dec 17 2019). V2.1 includes an update to:

  • structured dose direction cluster
  • structured dose amount
  • structured dose timing and dose direction duration.
