Discharging someone from mental health services to another care setting

Description

A person who has recieved mental health treatment is to be discharged. This standard ensures the infromation about the discharge is collected and sent consistently so that at a later date, if that same person goes for treatment in another care setting the care provider in that setting can easily access the information from that discharge.

Related standards and formats

Mental health inpatient discharge
Standardising information shared so professionals can provide continuity of care when an adult is discharged from mental health services. Includes information on patient history, medications, as well as current and previous diagnoses.
