Community

The Standards Directory is open to anyone who uses, develops, commissions, owns or assures standards and APIs relating to interoperable technology in health and social care. Here you will find resources to get involved or find out more about interoperability in health and social care.

Contact the standards directory team if you have any questions.

Resources

INTEROPen forum

INTEROPen is an open collaboration of individuals, industry, standards organisations and health and care providers to discuss, share knowledge and solve issues around standards development, implementation and adoption. To join INTEROPen please contact membership@interopen.org and access will be granted to the INTEROPen forum hosted on Ryver.

NHS Service Standard

The NHS Service Standard is designed to help teams address the needs of health service users. Understand why making your service interoperable is important to help navigate the complexities of delivering digital services in health and care.
